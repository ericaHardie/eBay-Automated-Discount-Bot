## Introduction
The **eBay Automated Discount Bot** streamlines your discount strategy by continuously monitoring listings, identifying underperforming products, and applying optimized discount rules automatically.  
It removes the hassle of manual discount updates, helping sellers maintain competitiveness while increasing conversion rates.

### Automating eBay Price Adjustments
- Automatically detects products with low sales or high stock.
- Applies smart discount percentages based on real-time performance.
- Syncs directly with eBay APIs or through Android automation.
- Increases sales while preserving profit margins.
- Eliminates manual discount management and human error.

## FAQs
**Q1: Can I connect multiple eBay accounts?**  
Yes, the bot supports multiple account configurations and session management within the Appilot dashboard.

**Q2: Does it integrate directly with eBay APIs?**  
It can either use direct API calls or perform Android UI automation through Appilot, depending on setup preference.

**Q3: Can I schedule discounts for specific days?**  
Yes, you can automate flash sales, holiday campaigns, and weekly discounts easily through the scheduler module.

**Q4: Is proxy rotation supported?**  
Yes, proxy rotation is built-in to avoid repetitive IP patterns during automated sessions.
